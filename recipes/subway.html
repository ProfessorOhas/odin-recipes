Subway

Description

Subway is an American multi-national fast food restaurant franchise that primarily sells submarine sandwiches (subs), wraps, salads and beverages. Subway was founded by 17 year old Fred DeLuca and financed by Peter Buck in 1965 as Pete's Super Submarines in Bridgeport, Connecticut.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Any choice of veggies
  • Meat or veggie patty

Steps

  1. Toast bread and cheese.
  2. Cut bread in half, hotdog style.
  3. Place toasted cheese in bread.
  4. Put either any choice of meat or a veggy patty ontop of the cheese.
  5. Place any choice of veggies ontop of either they veggy patty or the meat.
  6. Lastly, eat and enjoy!
