Taco

Description

Tacos… a Mexican dish consisting of a fried tortilla, typically folded, filled with various mixtures, such as seasoned meat, beans, lettuce, and tomatoes, but what does it really take to make the perfect taco? Tacos are an easy dish that the Quik n’ Crispy cooks to perfection, so check out these five easy steps to the best taco you’ve ever had.

Ingredients

  • Taco shell
  • Beans
  • Meat (optional)
  • Sour cream
  • Cheese
  • Guacamole
  • Salsa
  • Veggies (tomatoes, lettuce)

Steps

  1. Place taco shell on plate.
  2. Put beans (and meat) inside of the taco shell.
  3. Put sour cream, cheese, and guacamole into the shell.
  4. Place salsa and any veggies into the salsa.
  5. Lastly, eat and enjoy!
